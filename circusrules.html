Rules of the Cirque


THE RULES

This Cirqe is Covid 19 secure. All guests must wear masks.
Follow all social distancing guidelines.
Do not enter any areas marked staff only.

Lost children should be taken to the nearest clown.
We will take care of them.

Do not approach or try to feed the animals.
In case of fire, evacuate by the nearest exit.

If you hear crying in the dark report
it to Baroness Gardner directly.

All guests must keep their tickets with them.

If you hear the spiders RUN! If you can see them then it is too late...
